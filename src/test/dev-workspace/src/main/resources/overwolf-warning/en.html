Overwolf Compatibility Warning

Important Notice

We have detected that Overwolf is currently running on your system. Overwolf can cause severe issues with LabyMod!

Common problems that may occur include:

  • Game crashes
  • Freezing of LabyMod
  • Significant performance drops

Recommended Action

We recommend closing Overwolf completely before starting LabyMod. To avoid problems in the future, it may also be useful to remove Overwolf from the autostart.

How to close Overwolf completely
Steps to fully close Overwolf:
  1. Right-click on the Overwolf icon in the taskbar (bottom right of your screen)
  2. Select "Quit" or "Exit" from the menu
  3. If Overwolf doesn't appear in your system tray, open Task Manager (Ctrl+Shift+Esc)
  4. Look for Overwolf processes in the list
  5. Select each Overwolf process and click "End Task"

Restart LabyMod after closing Overwolf. The problems should then be solved.

Why does this happen?

Overwolf “injects” itself into LabyMod - in simple terms, it changes how LabyMod and Minecraft work in the background from the outside. Overwolf does this in order to display overlays and read data from the game. However, this can cause errors that lead to crashes or freezes, as is currently the case. Unfortunately, LabyMod has no influence on these errors.
